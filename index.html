I am a PhD candidate at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore and a member of NUS Natural Language Processing Group under supervision of Prof. Ng Hwee Tou.

I have been working on Neural Networks, Deep Learning, Feature Learning and several Natural Language Processing applications, including Statistical Machine Translation (SMT), Word Sense Disambiguation (WSD), Word Sense Induction (WSI), Semantic Textual Similarity (STS), and Automated Essay Scoring (AES).
